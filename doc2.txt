Table Name: Tb_LE_code

Purpose:
Stores Legal Entity codes and descriptions.

Business Meaning:
Used to identify legal entities participating in financial transactions.

Columns:

LE_code
- Type: VARCHAR(20)
- Description: Unique legal entity code.

LE_desc
- Type: VARCHAR(255)
- Description: Description of the legal entity.

Relationships:
No foreign key relationships.

Primary Key:
LE_code
